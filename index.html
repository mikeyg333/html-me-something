Sports

Playing any kind of sport is by far my favorite kind of activity. I love being able to compete against someone else and it is a great way to stay in shape. It's difficult to say which is my absolute favorite to play, but I've listed some of the ones I've played recently.

  • Slow-pitch softball
  • Ice hockey
  • Flag football
  • Ultimate frisbee
  • Golf

Video Games

Another fun hobby of mine is playing video games. Some of my fondest memories growing up were sitting down in front of the TV with my sisters to play, whether we were taking turns trying to take down Bowser in the original Super Mario Bros at a very young age or competing against each other and having tons of laughs with Mario Party and Mario Kart as teenagers. I don't play them nearly as much as I used to ask a kid but I still find time to get some gaming sessions in every so often. Some of my all-time favorites are below.

  • The Legend of Zelda series
  • The Last Of Us
  • Super Mario 64
  • Goldeneye

Playing guitar

The last hobby of mine that I'll mention here is learning how to play guitar. Nearly everyone in my family knows how to played some type of musical instrument, but I never had much interest in learning as a child. I've always loved listening to different kinds of music and reading about the stories behind how artists / bands got started. In my college days, I read about one the bands I listened to frequently and how they taught themselves to play without being in a school band or taking lessons. This inspired me go out and buy a guitar and start trying to learn. It's been fun teaching myself but I still have a long way to go if I ever want to even come close as the guy in the picture above, Jimi Hendrix!
